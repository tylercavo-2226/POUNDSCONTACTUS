Why Diets Fail

You’re Not Failing Your Diet.
Your Diet Is Failing You.

Many of our patients arrive eating very little and exercising plenty — and still not losing weight. That’s not a willpower problem. That’s a biology problem.

Chronic stress triggers cortisol, which controls where fat gets stored, drives sugar cravings, and suppresses the thyroid hormones that regulate your metabolism. Most weight loss programs never test for it, let alone treat it.
