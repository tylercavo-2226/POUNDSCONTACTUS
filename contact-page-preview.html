Your First Appointment

Here's What We Actually Do — Start to Finish

Your first visit is not a consultation. It's a medical appointment.

1
Your History & What's Been Getting in the Way
One of our providers — Dr. Charlie Cavo, Michelle Cavo PA-C, or a member of our bariatrics-specialized clinical team — will sit down with you and go through your full history. What you've tried, what worked briefly, what stopped working, and what your body has been telling you that no one has listened to.
2
Comprehensive Metabolic Bloodwork
We run a full panel — insulin resistance, thyroid, cortisol, glucose, vitamin D, cholesterol, and more. Most of our patients have never had these run together. This is where we find what's actually been getting in the way.
3
A Plan Built Around Your Biology
Not a template. Not a meal plan printed from a website. A treatment plan built for your actual results — your life, your schedule, and what's realistically going to work for you long term.
